## Stat reading order

1. **Speed and initiative** decide first actions, blocks, and shooter protection.
2. **Damage range and attack** decide whether a target dies before it acts.
3. **Health and defense** decide whether retaliation is affordable.
4. **Movement and attack type** decide whether terrain, obstacles, and range penalties matter.
5. **Growth, cost, and experience** decide whether losses are acceptable for the map reward.
6. **Abilities** override simple math; always read them before taking high-loss neutral fights.
