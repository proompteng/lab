# Spells Reference

A spell is worth casting when it changes the fight or route. The visible effect matters less than the army saved, target killed, action denied, or movement unlocked.

## Spell roles

- Buff: makes your stack survive or kill at the right time.
- Debuff: makes the enemy miss a power spike.
- Damage: removes a dangerous stack or softens it for a clean kill.
- Control: denies movement, spellcasting, retaliation, or an ability window.
- Utility: changes map tempo, recovery, or scouting.

## Mana value

Do not spend mana just because a spell is available. Spend it when the current fight would otherwise cost important army or when the map reward is decisive.
