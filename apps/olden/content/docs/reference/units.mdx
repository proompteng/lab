# Units Reference

Units are not interchangeable damage numbers. Each stack has a job.

## Unit roles

- Damage stack: kills priority targets.
- Durable stack: absorbs hits and protects shooters.
- Ranged stack: deals damage while forcing enemy movement.
- Flyer or fast stack: blocks, reaches backlines, or claims tempo.
- Control stack: changes enemy movement, actions, or targeting.
- Sacrifice stack: protects important units by taking a low-value hit.

## What to check

- Speed and initiative decide who can act or block first.
- Damage range tells you whether a stack reliably kills the target.
- Health and defense decide whether retaliation is acceptable.
- Abilities decide if normal positioning rules change.
- Growth and cost decide how painful losses are.

## Beginner rule

Protect units that are hard to replace or essential to the next fight. Spending one cheap stack to save an elite stack is often correct.
