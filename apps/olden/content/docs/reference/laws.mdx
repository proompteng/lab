# Laws Reference

Laws are faction-specific development choices. They should reinforce the game you are actually playing, not an ideal game that the map did not offer.

## How to choose laws

- Pick economy laws when they accelerate dwellings, recruitment, or critical resources.
- Pick combat laws when the next fights decide territory.
- Pick magic or focus laws when abilities and spells are already central to your plan.
- Pick growth laws when they reach a timing before the opponent can punish it.

## Common mistake

Choosing a law because it sounds powerful while ignoring the map. A law that helps a late army does not save an opening that lacks mines or growth.
