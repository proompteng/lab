## How to read item value

- **Route items** are worth more before a long movement turn than after the hero has already spent movement.
- **Combat items** belong on the hero taking the next dangerous fight, not on the hero who found them.
- **Caster items** are only strong when mana, spell access, and spell power change the loss math.
- **Economy items** can sit on support heroes unless the main hero needs the slot for immediate survival or damage.
- **Set pieces** are not automatically correct: the partial pieces must still beat the best standalone item for the next route.
