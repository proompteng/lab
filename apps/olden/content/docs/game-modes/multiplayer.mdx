# Multiplayer

Multiplayer turns loose routing into immediate punishment. Human opponents race mines, threaten towns, and force final fights before a comfortable build is ready.

## Setup

Agree on map, timer, restart rules, and Early Access reload expectations before starting.

## Improvement focus

- Keep turns short and purposeful.
- Scout enemy direction earlier than in single-player.
- Protect town timing.
- Prepare the final fight before contact.
- Review first-week mistakes after the game.
