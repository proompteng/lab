# Native Terrain

Native terrain matters because faction armies are designed around a home environment and the map can reward or punish how quickly they operate there.

## Current faction terrains

- Temple: Grass
- Necropolis: Deathland
- Grove: Autumn
- Dungeon: Dirt
- Hive: Lava
- Schism: Snow

## Beginner rule

When two routes look equal, prefer the one where your army moves and fights naturally while preserving movement for the next object.

## Gameplay use

Terrain knowledge helps with scouting, chase decisions, defense planning, and predicting which opponent can reach a key area first. It also helps when judging random map starts: a beautiful resource pile on awkward terrain may cost more than it looks.
