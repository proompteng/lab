# Siege Combat

Siege combat changes normal fight math because town defenses, chokepoints, and delayed access can protect the defender.

## Attacking

Attack when the town value, timing, or opponent denial is worth the expected losses. If waiting gives the defender another week of growth, a costly siege may still be correct.

## Defending

Defend when the town contains recruitment, economy, or faction mechanics that cannot be replaced quickly. Sometimes the correct defense is forcing the attacker to spend enough army that your next hero can punish.

## Beginner rule

Do not attack a town because it is visible. Attack because taking it changes the game.
