# Towns

Towns convert resources into weekly power. They also control territory, protect recruitment, and define which faction mechanics are available.

## Beginner rule

Build growth first unless a different building immediately unlocks a critical route, spell, defense, or faction mechanic.

## Town decisions

- Creature dwellings create recurring value.
- Upgrades matter when they change speed, survivability, ability access, or damage breakpoints.
- Economy buildings matter when they pay back before the game is decided.
- Magic buildings matter when spells change fights more than extra bodies.
- Defense matters when the opponent can reach the town before your hero returns.

## Common mistake

Building as if every game lasts forever. In tempo games, a late payoff building may lose to a dwelling or spell that wins this week.
