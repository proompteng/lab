# Creature Growth

Creature growth is the recurring supply of army. The earlier a dwelling is online, the more weeks it contributes.

## Beginner rule

Do not lose hard-to-replace stacks for low-value rewards. Growth is powerful only if the army survives long enough to combine with it.

## Growth timing

A dwelling built before the weekly rollover can matter immediately. A dwelling built after the rollover may effectively be one week late. This is why early resource routing and town timing are tightly connected.

## Preservation

Army preservation is not passive play. It lets the main hero take more fights, claim more mines, and reach higher-value objectives without stopping.
