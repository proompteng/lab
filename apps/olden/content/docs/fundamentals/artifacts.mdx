# Artifacts

Artifacts matter because they shift breakpoints: a stack survives one more hit, a hero reaches one more tile, a spell becomes available, or a fight becomes cheap enough to take early.

## Beginner rule

Value an artifact by the next two fights or route timings it changes.

## Evaluation questions

- Does this improve the main hero or only a support hero?
- Does it save army in the next critical fight?
- Does it unlock a spell, speed, morale, luck, or damage breakpoint?
- Does it belong on a scout until the main hero needs it?
- Is the guarded artifact worth the army required to claim it?

## Common mistake

Taking a hard artifact fight because the item looks rare. If the losses delay a town, mine, or week-two timing, the artifact may be a trap.
