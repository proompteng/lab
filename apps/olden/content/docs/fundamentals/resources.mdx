# Resources

Resources are timing tools. A resource pile matters because it unlocks a building, recruit, spell, artifact fight, or recovery path.

## Resource jobs

- Gold buys heroes, recruits, and recovery.
- Wood and ore unlock basic development.
- Rare resources unlock faction-specific buildings, magic, or elite plans.
- Mines turn one fight into repeated income.

## Beginner rule

If two rewards are close, prefer the one that removes tomorrow's building bottleneck.

## Common mistake

Taking a hard fight for a resource that is not currently blocking anything. The loss may matter more than the pile.
