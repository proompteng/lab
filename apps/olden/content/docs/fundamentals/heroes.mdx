# Heroes

Heroes carry armies, cast spells, claim objects, scout, and turn experience into permanent power. A hero is not just a stat block; it is a route and combat plan.

## Hero jobs

- Main fighter: takes dangerous battles and carries the best army.
- Scout: reveals fog, collects safe resources, watches enemy movement.
- Chain support: moves troops, artifacts, or information between towns and the main hero.
- Specialist: exists for a specific economy, spell, faction, or map role.

## Beginner rule

Do not split your real army between too many heroes. One strong main hero clears more safely than three weak heroes losing stacks everywhere.

## Leveling

Level choices should support the hero job. A main fighter needs combat reliability, army preservation, and spell access. A scout needs movement and survival. A support hero should not steal the army needed for critical fights.
