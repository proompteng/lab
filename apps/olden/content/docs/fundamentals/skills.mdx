# Skills

Skills define what a hero becomes. They can improve movement, combat, magic, economy, scouting, or faction-specific plans.

## Beginner rule

Pick skills that support the job already assigned to the hero. Do not turn a main fighter into a weak scout or a scout into a half-built combat hero by accident.

## Main hero priorities

- Army preservation.
- Reliable damage or control.
- Movement and logistics when the map demands speed.
- Spell access that matches the faction plan.
- Economy only when it accelerates a real timing.

## Scout priorities

Scouts value movement, vision, survivability, and cheap utility. They should reveal information without stealing the main army's growth path.
