# Magic

Magic changes fights by preventing losses, creating burst damage, controlling enemy actions, improving movement, or enabling routes that the army could not take cleanly.

## Beginner rule

The best spell is often the one that saves the most future army, not the one with the largest damage number.

## Spell roles

- Damage: removes or weakens a dangerous stack.
- Buffs: make your key stack survive, move, or hit harder.
- Debuffs: make enemy power spikes miss their timing.
- Control: denies the action that would swing the fight.
- Map utility: changes movement, scouting, recovery, or route planning.

## Mana discipline

Do not spend mana to make a safe fight slightly faster if the next fight needs that mana to avoid losses. Conversely, do not hoard mana while losing troops that cannot be replaced.
