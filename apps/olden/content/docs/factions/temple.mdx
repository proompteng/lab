# Temple

Temple is the cleanest fundamentals faction: balanced units, positive effects, durable lines, and reliable combat roles. It teaches how to protect ranged damage, use buffs before losses happen, and turn stable town growth into a better week two.

## How Temple wins

Temple wins by preserving army while taking steady map value. Its best games look controlled rather than explosive: mines arrive on time, key stacks survive neutral fights, and buffs turn fair trades into favorable trades.

## First week

- Claim wood, ore, and gold routes early.
- Build toward recurring growth before luxury upgrades.
- Keep shooters and fragile value stacks protected.
- Take fights where buffs prevent losses, not fights where buffs merely reduce a bad trade.

## Combat

Temple wants an orderly formation. Let durable stacks absorb pressure, use ranged and elite stacks to remove threats, and commit flyers only when blocking or killing changes the round.

## Counterplay

Force Temple to split attention. If its buffs cover only one side of the fight, pressure the other. On the map, punish slow routing before Temple reaches stable growth.
