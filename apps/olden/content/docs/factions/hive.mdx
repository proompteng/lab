# Hive

Hive is a demonic insectoid swarm faction. Its army is strongest when the pieces reinforce each other and the player commits during the pressure window.

## How Hive wins

Hive wins by keeping synergy intact and forcing the opponent to answer before their ideal economy, spell, or elite stack timing is ready.

## First week

- Keep the core army together until the synergy plan is clear.
- Take fights that preserve enough stack mass for the next fight.
- Build for pressure, not for a disconnected long-game fantasy.
- Avoid overcommitting one stack without follow-up.

## Combat

Use speed and numbers to create first-hit value. Commit when multiple stacks can exploit the same target. If the army gets thinned too early, the faction loses the pressure identity that makes it dangerous.

## Counterplay

Break tempo with blockers, terrain, and defensive trades. Survive the pressure window, then punish spent stacks and broken synergy.
