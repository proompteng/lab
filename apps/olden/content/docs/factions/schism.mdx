# Schism

Schism is a control faction tied to the Depths of Water. It denies enemy abilities and magic while using summons and battlefield control to turn equal fights into awkward fights for the opponent.

## How Schism wins

Schism wins by spending denial on the action that would have swung the round. The faction is not trying to delay forever; it must convert disabled turns into kills, saved stacks, or objective tempo.

## First week

- Fight manually to learn which actions are worth denying.
- Preserve real army with summons and control.
- Develop the hero toward decisive control turns.
- Avoid spending control just because it is available.

## Combat

Identify the enemy's decisive next action. Deny it, then use the free window to kill or block the stack that matters. If the fight is only delayed and not improved, the control was mistimed.

## Counterplay

Present multiple threats so Schism cannot deny all of them. Bait control on a medium threat, then commit the real damage. Force material trades before the control loop stabilizes.
