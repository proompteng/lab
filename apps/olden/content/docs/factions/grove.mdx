# Grove

Grove is a nature and fae faction about movement, growth, and flexible map pressure. It rewards players who scout early and choose fights because the route is good, not because the nearest object is available.

## How Grove wins

Grove wins by making movement advantages matter. Good Grove play connects town timing, terrain, and fights so the army arrives prepared while the opponent is still reacting.

## First week

- Reveal roads and route branches quickly.
- Keep the main army together until the first growth and mine timings are stable.
- Use map-flow tools to claim resources without exposing the main hero.
- Avoid specialist-stack losses in low-value fights.

## Combat

Protect fragile or specialist units until they generate tempo. Grove should choose fights where its movement and positioning create favorable trades before raw army count decides everything.

## Counterplay

Make Grove defend fixed points. Pressure towns, mines, or timing windows so movement options shrink and split armies are punished.
