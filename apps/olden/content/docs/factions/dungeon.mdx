# Dungeon

Dungeon is a high-ceiling faction built around dark elves, underground creatures, flexible attacks, and decisive tactical trades. It rewards precision and punishes casual positioning.

## How Dungeon wins

Dungeon wins by making a fight unfair. Flexible attack patterns and dangerous premium stacks turn small positioning mistakes into large losses for the opponent.

## First week

- Scout enough to choose fights where tactical options matter.
- Keep expensive stacks protected.
- Build toward the core army before buying every upgrade.
- Avoid fights where the enemy gets clean retaliation into your best damage.

## Combat

Attack from angles that deny follow-up. Commit elite damage only after checking the next enemy action. If a flexible attack can kill a threat without opening your own stack, that is usually the Dungeon line.

## Counterplay

Keep formations compact and deny premium angles. Pressure Dungeon economy because mistakes are expensive, and trade into key stacks before perfect positioning is available.
