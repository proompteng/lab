# Necropolis

Necropolis is an undead attrition faction. It wants fights that leave the enemy depleted while its own plan keeps compounding through preserved bodies, reanimation value, and morale-neutral consistency.

## How Necropolis wins

Necropolis turns efficient fights into future pressure. When the first week is clean, the faction can keep fighting while opponents need to rest, rebuild, or avoid army trades.

## First week

- Take neutral fights that feed the engine without destroying key stacks.
- Keep the main hero moving through connected rewards.
- Do not confuse many fights with good fights; bad attrition still slows you.
- Build enough economy to recruit the army that makes the snowball matter.

## Combat

Let expendable bodies protect important stacks. Remove enemy units that threaten future fight efficiency. Avoid spending premium stacks when a slower, safer line gets the same reward.

## Counterplay

Deny easy chains. Force Necropolis into expensive fights or objective races before its snowball becomes material. Kill the stacks that make future fights cheap.
