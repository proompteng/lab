# Glossary

## Adventure map

The strategic map where heroes move, collect resources, claim mines, visit objects, and start battles.

## Creeping

Clearing neutral enemies for rewards while preserving army.

## Dwellings

Town or map structures that provide creature growth.

## Growth

The recurring weekly supply of recruitable creatures.

## Main hero

The hero carrying the strongest army and taking the decisive fights.

## Native terrain

The terrain associated with a faction's identity and movement/combat expectations.

## Power spike

A timing where a hero, army, spell, building, or artifact makes a faction temporarily much stronger.

## Tempo

The speed at which map actions become material advantage.
