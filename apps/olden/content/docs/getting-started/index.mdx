# Getting Started

Olden Era rewards players who turn limited movement into resources, resources into towns, towns into armies, and armies into cleaner fights. New players should learn one faction, one map size, and one win condition before chasing perfect routes.

## First priorities

1. Pick Temple for the clearest learning pass, or pick the faction whose fantasy keeps you engaged.
2. Reveal nearby mines, roads, creature dwellings, and safe fights before wandering.
3. Build economy and creature growth before spending everything on upgrades.
4. Keep elite stacks alive. Losing key stacks early usually costs more than skipping a risky fight.
5. Learn one combat idea at a time: initiative, retaliation, ranged pressure, spell timing, blocking, and focus abilities.

## What to ignore at first

Do not try to memorize every artifact, hero, or spell before playing. Learn the map loop first. A player who collects mines on time and preserves army will beat a player who knows more trivia but loses tempo.
