# PvP Basics

Human opponents punish loose turns. PvP is not only a better combat test; it is a better tempo test.

## Before the match

Agree on map, timer, restart rules, allowed settings, and whether the match is casual practice or competitive.

## During the match

- Keep turns purposeful.
- Scout enemy direction.
- Protect towns and recruitment timing.
- Do not expose the main hero after a costly fight.
- Track whether the opponent can reach a contested object before you.

## Review

When replays or observer tools are available, review the first missed timing rather than only the final battle. Most final battles are decided by earlier map turns.
