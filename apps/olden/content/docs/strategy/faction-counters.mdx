# Faction Counters

Counterplay starts by identifying what the opponent needs to make their faction work.

## Counter principles

- Against Temple: split pressure so buffs cannot cover every threat.
- Against Necropolis: deny cheap attrition chains and force expensive fights.
- Against Grove: make it defend fixed points and punish split armies.
- Against Dungeon: keep formation compact and deny premium attack angles.
- Against Hive: survive the pressure window and break synergy stack mass.
- Against Schism: present multiple threats so denial cannot stop every key action.

## Map counterplay

Factions are not countered only in battle. You can counter a faction by stealing its mine timing, forcing a town defense, blocking a road, or making its main hero choose between recruitment and pressure.

## Combat counterplay

Do not fight the faction fantasy directly. Fight the condition that enables it. If Hive needs stack mass, thin it. If Schism needs one denied action to matter, present two threats. If Dungeon wants a perfect angle, deny the angle.
