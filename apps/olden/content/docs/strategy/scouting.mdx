# Scouting

Scouting turns hidden map risk into route decisions. A good scout saves the main hero from walking into dead ends, bad fights, or late objectives.

## What scouts reveal

- Mine and dwelling positions.
- Road branches and terrain cost.
- Enemy hero direction.
- Guard strength around important rewards.
- Safe loose resources that do not need the main army.

## Scout discipline

Do not give scouts the army needed by the main hero unless the transfer creates a real timing. A scout that survives and reveals is doing the job.

## Common mistake

Scouting only after the main hero has already committed. Good scouting happens one or two turns before the decision.
