# Recovery Plans

Recovery starts by naming the actual problem. Losing army, missing a mine, losing movement, and picking the wrong fight require different repairs.

## If you lost army

Stop taking marginal fights. Recruit, regroup, and use spells or safer routes to rebuild a fight-capable stack.

## If you missed economy

Identify the bottleneck resource and route to the fastest safe source. Do not buy upgrades that deepen the bottleneck.

## If the opponent is faster

Defend fixed points, threaten a town trade, or take a high-value objective they cannot ignore. Chasing directly often loses more movement.

## If the hero build is wrong

Give that hero a narrower job. A flawed main hero may still be useful as a support, scout, or defensive holder while another hero carries the next objective.
