# Creeping

Creeping means clearing neutral fights for rewards. Good creeping is not about winning every possible fight. It is about winning the right fights cheaply.

## Before the fight

- Identify the enemy stack that can kill your important units.
- Check whether your ranged or fast stack acts before it.
- Decide which unit can safely absorb retaliation.
- Know what reward the fight unlocks.

## During the fight

Protect premium stacks. Use cheap bodies, terrain, control, and spell timing to prevent losses. If a spell saves a stack that enables the next fight, cast it.

## After the fight

Evaluate losses against route value. Losing a few cheap units for a mine can be excellent. Losing an elite stack for a loose pile is usually bad.
