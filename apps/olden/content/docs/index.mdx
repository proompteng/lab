## Core idea

Olden Era is a compounding game. A turn spent walking badly becomes a mine captured late. A mine captured late becomes a dwelling delayed. A dwelling delayed becomes fewer troops. Fewer troops make neutral fights cost more. Costly neutral fights slow the next route. Good play breaks that chain early.
