## Hero jobs

- **Main hero** carries the best army, takes the route-defining fights, and needs loss reduction, tempo, and spell value.
- **Scout** reveals map, chains safe resources, and keeps movement pressure without stealing core troops.
- **Caster/support hero** turns mana and spell access into safer fights or utility.
- **Economy hero** matters only when the payoff arrives before the decisive build or fight.
